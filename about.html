About Sweet Boost

Sweet Boost

Sweet Boost is a unique nutrition bar made with wholesome ingredients like sweet potatoes, honey, oats, peanuts, and walnuts. It's a convenient and nutritious snack for anyone looking for a quick boost of essential nutrients. Plus, Sweet Boost supports the Zero Hunger Goal of the United Nations.

More About Sweet Boost

We are located in the beautiful city of Hermosillo, Sonora. We specialize in creating delicious sweets and treats that are sure to satisfy any sweet tooth. We believe that high-quality ingredients and attention to detail are key to creating exceptional products.

Market Analysis

Sweet Boost protein bar is a delicious and nutritious energy boost for athletes, professionals, and health-conscious individuals.

Ingredients

Made with the best ingredients, such as sweet potatoes, honey, oats, peanuts and walnuts. Contains carbohydrates, vitamins A, E and C, antioxidants, fiber and potassium.

Benefits / Advantages

Sweet Boost offers several benefits such as being a healthy and sustainable source of food, packed with essential nutrients and protein. It's also convenient for on-the-go snacking or emergency aid situations due to its long shelf life and easy storage. Its natural ingredients provide a delicious, guilt-free treat that can keep you energized throughout the day.

Zero Hunger Relationship

Sweet Boost can help achieve the Zero Hunger Goal of the United Nations. Packed with essential nutrients and protein, this bar provides a healthy and sustainable source of food for people in need, particularly in regions where food scarcity is an issue.

About Us

We are actually students of HighPoint International School Hermosillo. We are working on this project because we want to contribute the ONU's Second Goal: Zero Hunger, we belive Sweet Boost is a very promising product and we hope it goes a long way.
